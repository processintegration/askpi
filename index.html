About AskPI

Process Integration is a key component in the chemical engineering curriculum, particularly for sustainable energy and resource optimization. Despite its importance, undergraduate students often struggle with its abstract concepts and analytical demands.

Drawing from five years of teaching experience, AskPI addresses these challenges by combining visual learning tools with interactive AI support to enhance conceptual understanding and independent problem-solving.

This Open Educational Resource (OER) promotes active learning, self-paced study, and continuous support outside the traditional classroom through three interconnected components.

Learning Resources

Visual Concept Cards

Single-topic explanations using intuitive diagrams, simplified equations, real-world analogies, and QR-linked microlearning videos.

These cards serve as concise reference tools for both classroom use and self-study.

Guided Worksheets

Scaffold student learning by breaking down complex problems into manageable steps with conceptual warm-ups, graphical plotting activities, and reflective questions.

Guide learners through the logic of Process Integration analysis step by step.

AI-Powered Learning Assistant

The AskPI AI assistant functions as a virtual tutor, offering clarity on frequently misunderstood topics and walking students through typical Process Integration problems in a conversational format.

This feature is especially valuable for students who prefer self-paced or asynchronous learning environments.
