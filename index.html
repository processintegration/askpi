AskPI Overview

About AskPI

Welcome to AskPI, your open-source companion for mastering Process Integration in chemical engineering!

Process Integration (PI) is a vital part of chemical engineering, especially when it comes to sustainable energy and resource optimization.

However, many students find its concepts challenging due to their abstract nature and the complex analytical tasks involved. AskPI helps by providing a more accessible, flexible, and learner-centric approach.

What is AskPI?

AskPI is an Open Educational Resource (OER) designed to transform how undergraduate students learn and engage with Process Integration.

Our primary goal is to enhance your conceptual understanding and empower your independent problem-solving skills through a unique blend of visual learning tools and interactive AI support.

Our Core Components
  • Visual Concept Cards: Break down complex ideas using intuitive diagrams, simplified equations, and key notes.
  • Guided Worksheets: Step-by-step guides with warm-ups, plots, and reflections for deeper understanding.
  • AI-Powered Q&A: Mobile-friendly tutor answering questions in real-time, trained on student materials.
Why AskPI is Different

AskPI integrates visual pedagogy with AI. It's built using accessible tools (PowerPoint, Word, HTML), easily adaptable, and LMS-friendly.

Our Impact

AskPI boosts understanding, confidence, and assessment performance. Licensed under Creative Commons to support open education everywhere.
